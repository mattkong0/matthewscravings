BOBA




Yifang Taiwan Tea

With many locations within the SF Bay Area, Yifang is very much known for it's Brown Sugar Pearl Latte.



  • Matcha Brown Sugar Pearl Latte: top-notch

  • Honey boba is so CHEWY and so GOOD when it's fresh

  • Recommend Brown Sugar Pearl Latte or Matcha Brown Sugar Pearl Latte









Snow/Mini Monster

Located in Southern California, Snow Monster notoriously serves their drinks in large glass bottles and topped with cotton candy.



  • Cute reusable jars available for purchase (extra $2-$3)

  • Drinks are very creative

  • Recommend Sunset Strawberry Lemonade w/ Boba or Thai Tea w/ Boba





Tpumps

With locations in SF, Burlingame, Foster City, and more, Tpumps is full of combinations. You can pick up to 3 flavors.



  • Best part = pick up to 3 flavors

  • Boba is chewy.

  • Recommend Mango Peach Passion GT (light sweet)
