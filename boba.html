BOBA




Camellia Rd Tea Bar

Located in San Diego, Camellia Rd is well-known for having high-quality tea.



  • My personal favorite boba spot in San Diego

  • Boba is so chewy when it's fresh

  • Little bit expensive, but so worth it









Snow/Mini Monster

Located in Southern California, Snow Monster is known for their big glass bottles and drinks with cotton candy on top of the bottle.



  • They have resuable jars. (They cost extra $2-$3)

  • Drinks are very creative

  • Recommend Sunset Strawberry Lemonade w/ Boba or Thai Tea w/ Boba





Boba Guys

With locations in SF, LA County, and New York City, Boba Guys puts a twist on the classic Bubble Tea.



  • Higher price point, but worth the wait

  • The boba is chewy and fresh

  • Personal Favorite: Strawberry Matcha Latte, which is also their most popular drink
