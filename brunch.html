BRUNCH


Kitchen Story

Located on the corner of 16th and Sanchez, Kitchen Story is one of the best brunch spots in the Castro District of San Francisco.



  • Long lines are common and it is a little pricey, but definitely worth the wait!

  • Fresh and colorful food presentation

  • Personal favorite: Ribeye Omurice









Ellen's

Located in Dallas, Texas, this Southern Kitchen puts its own twist on breakfast classics.



  • Biscuits and Gravy are top-notch

  • Good quality service

  • Recommend Big Ol' Breakfast





Squat and Gobble

Located in San Francisco's West Portal and Marina Districts, Squat and Gobble's locations are highly popular for their savory and sweet crepes!



  • Easy to get to by metro or bus

  • Good quality service

  • I recommend the Triple Gobble
