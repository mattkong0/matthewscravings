COFFEE




Starbucks

With many locations across the globe, Starbucks is a major hub for work and just somewhere to grad a quick bite during your break.



  • Free drinks on your birthday

  • Fast service

  • Recommend Caramel Macchiato








Philz Coffee

With many locations in the Bay Area, Philz is the place where people get their coffee and do their work.



  • Coffee is top-notch

  • Fast service

  • Recommend Mint Mojito





Antigua Coffee

Located on Taraval Street in San Francisco, this small petite coffee shop is the place to be for a quick bite or if you need a place to do your work.



  • Good place to do your work

  • Good quality coffee

  • Recommend Iced Vanilla Latte
