INTERNATIONAL




Giovanni

Located in Paris, France, this Italian pizzeria serves some of the best pizza in Paris.



  • The pizzas are really tasty

  • Good service

  • Recommend Spaghetti Carbonara











Fuller's Ale and Pie House

With multiple locations in London, this classic British Pub is well known for their pie and mash.



  • Fish n Chips was 3.5/5

  • Good service

  • Recommend their handcrafted pies/mini pies





Terrazza Dei Nobili

Located in Venice, Italy, this classic Italian restaurant has the best views of the ocean.



  • Outdoor dining recommended if you want the views

  • Decent service

  • Recommend Squid Ink Spaghetti
