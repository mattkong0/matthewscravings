RAMEN




Menya Ultra Ramen

With multiple locations in San Diego, Menya Ultra serves authentic Japanese Ramen and takes it to a whole new level.



  • Long lines are expected, but it is definitely worth the wait

  • Fast service

  • Personal recommendation: Negi Miso Ramen









Ajisen Ramen

With many locations in the Bay Area, Ajisen has Ramen like no other. They are best known for their Premium Pork Ramen.



  • High quality Ramen

  • Fast service

  • Recommend ABC (Ajisen Best Combo) Ramen





Marufuku Ramen SF

Located in Japantown of San Francisco, Marufuku serves high quality ramen as they are one of the best ramen places in SF.



  • Ramen is very flavorful

  • Perfect portion even though it is little bit more expensive

  • Recommend Hakata Tonkotsu DX Ramen
