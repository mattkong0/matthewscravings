MY RECOMMENDATIONS




Lou's Sandwiches

Located in San Francisco, it is one of the many hotspots for people who just want to grab a bite and head back to school before class.



  • Great sandwiches

  • Lots of options

  • Recommend Italian Special







Marugame Udon

With locations in California and Hawaii, Marugame Udon puts on a show for their customers by giving them the authentic Japanese experience.



  • Love the traditional Japanese experience

  • Somewhat High price point

  • Recommend Tonkotsu Udon or Nikutama Udon





Golden Island Cafe

Located on Noriega Street in San Francisco, Golden Island is the place to grab a quick bite or just to hangout.



  • Typical Hangout Spot for Lincoln students

  • Fast service

  • Recommend their Mango Sago
