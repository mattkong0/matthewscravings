San Diego




Oscar's Mexican Seafood

With two locations in Pacific Beach, Oscar's is known for their high-quality tacos.



  • Very expensive price point

  • Good portions

  • Recommend Smoked Fish Tacos










Nishiki Ramen

Located in Kearny Mesa, Nishiki Ramen takes authentic Japanese Ramen and puts a SoCal twist.



  • Good portions

  • Good price point

  • Recommend Smoke Black Bomb Ramen





Blue Box Bagels and Brew

Located in Mira Mesa, Blue Box is the perfect place to get your work done or just grab a quick bite.



  • Affordable price point

  • Bagels are super delicious

  • Recommend Golden Scramble
